The Journey

London-based DJ and producer SMITH (UK) has spent nearly two decades at the heart of the house music scene, cultivating a sound defined by rolling grooves, soulful jazz undertones, and a sharp, rhythmic nod to speed garage.

With 17 years of experience behind the decks, SMITH's technical proficiency is matched only by his curation. Whether navigating the timeless feel of vinyl or modern digital setups, his sets are built for the floor—deep, warm, and relentlessly upbeat.

Transitioning from his previous alias (S.M.I.T.H) to his current identity, the SMITH (UK) project represents a refined evolution of his craft. His production output is a testament to this, consistently hitting the upper echelons of the Beatport charts.
